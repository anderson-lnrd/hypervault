Radio Button

Grupo de radio buttons com suporte a ControlValueAccessor, cores, layout horizontal/vertical e label position.

Basico

Radio group simples com opcoes verticais.

Maca Banana Cereja

Selecionado: {{ selectedFruit() }}

<hyper-radio-group [(value)]="selectedFruit">
  <hyper-radio-button value="apple">Maca</hyper-radio-button>
  <hyper-radio-button value="banana">Banana</hyper-radio-button>
  <hyper-radio-button value="cherry">Cereja</hyper-radio-button>
</hyper-radio-group>

Layout Horizontal

Use layout="horizontal" para dispor as opcoes em linha.

Pequeno Medio Grande 
<hyper-radio-group layout="horizontal" value="sm">
  <hyper-radio-button value="sm">Pequeno</hyper-radio-button>
  <hyper-radio-button value="md">Medio</hyper-radio-button>
  <hyper-radio-button value="lg">Grande</hyper-radio-button>
</hyper-radio-group>

Cores

Cores disponiveis: primary (padrao) e secondary.

Primary A Primary B Secondary A Secondary B 
<hyper-radio-group color="secondary" layout="horizontal" value="a">
  <hyper-radio-button value="a">Secondary A</hyper-radio-button>
  <hyper-radio-button value="b">Secondary B</hyper-radio-button>
</hyper-radio-group>

Label Position

Use labelPosition="before" para posicionar o label antes do radio.

Opcao 1 Opcao 2 
<hyper-radio-group labelPosition="before" value="opt1">
  <hyper-radio-button value="opt1">Opcao 1</hyper-radio-button>
  <hyper-radio-button value="opt2">Opcao 2</hyper-radio-button>
</hyper-radio-group>

Desabilitado

Desabilite o grupo inteiro ou opcoes individuais.

Grupo Disabled Opcao Y Habilitado Individual Disabled 
<!-- Grupo inteiro -->
<hyper-radio-group [disabled]="true" value="x">...</hyper-radio-group>

<!-- Opcao individual -->
<hyper-radio-button value="b" [disabled]="true">Disabled</hyper-radio-button>

Com Formularios

Implementa ControlValueAccessor, compativel com ngModel e Reactive Forms.

<hyper-radio-group [(value)]="selected">...</hyper-radio-group>

// Reactive Forms
<hyper-radio-group [formControl]="myControl">
  <hyper-radio-button value="opt1">Opcao 1</hyper-radio-button>
</hyper-radio-group>
