Badge

Componente de badge para rotulos, status e gamificacao. Suporta variantes de cor, sistema de raridade e icones inline.

Variantes

Quatro variantes base: default (primaria), secondary, outline e destructive.

Default Secondary Outline Destructive 
<hyper-badge>Default</hyper-badge>
<hyper-badge variant="secondary">Secondary</hyper-badge>
<hyper-badge variant="outline">Outline</hyper-badge>
<hyper-badge variant="destructive">Destructive</hyper-badge>

Badges de Raridade

Sistema de gamificacao com quatro niveis: common, rare, epic e legendary. Quando rarity e definido, sobrepoe a variante.

Comum Raro Epico Lendario 
<hyper-badge rarity="common">Comum</hyper-badge>
<hyper-badge rarity="rare">Raro</hyper-badge>
<hyper-badge rarity="epic">Epico</hyper-badge>
<hyper-badge rarity="legendary">Lendario</hyper-badge>
